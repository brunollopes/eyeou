ABOUT

EYEOU is a web platform having available photographic contests made from professional photographers to all around photographers.

It aims to provide this premium service where anyone can use it test their photographic skills by participating on the available contests. Each contest has a specific time for submission and afterwards the review done by the judge, which is set by at least 3 members of our ambassadors. For all the details please check the rules.

Our team of ambassadors reflects the premium quality of these contests, we have with us the well known and best of professional photographers in Portugal and also with great references overseas, including the portuguese number 1 as traveler photographer.
